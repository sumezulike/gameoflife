The Four Rules

Every cell in this infinite grid follows four simple rules each generation:

  2. Survival: A live cell with two or three neighbors survives
  3. Underpopulation: A live cell with fewer than two neighbors dies (loneliness)
  4. Overpopulation: A live cell with more than three neighbors dies (overcrowding)
